All the 66 Books in the Bible

The length of the Bible is about 611,000 words spread across 66 shorter texts known as its "books"—is enormous.This is so because the Bible is a compilation of texts from several authors who lived in various eras. We can read the Bible in "chunks" rather than having to read it all at once, which in some ways makes it simpler to approach. However, it also adds some confusion. The Bible is a book unto itself. The Latin and Greek words for "book" (biblia and biblos, respectively) are actually where the word "bible" originates. It's a collection of books, though. Here is a summary of each Bible book.
