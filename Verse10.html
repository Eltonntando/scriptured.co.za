Love is the last thing we feel when we are discouraged. Loving others and loving ourselves is the most difficult thing when we are in the middle of a storm. But, Scripture reminds us that God is love and that we are called to love others. May these encouraging Bible verses remind you that you are a child of God who is loved with an everlasting love!

"Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud. It is does not dishonor other, it is not self-seeking, it is not easily engered, it keeps no record of wrongs. Love does not delight in evil but rejoices with the truth. It always protects, always trusts, always hopes, always perseveres. Love never fails.
